Iced latte

A refreshing iced latte recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 espresso shots (60ml)
  • 2 tsp sugar or honey
  • ice
  • 110ml milk

Steps

  1. Mix the hot espresso with the sugar.
  2. Fill a glass with ice and stir in the coffee.
  3. Pour over the milk and stir.
